# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to a respectful, focused, and harassment-free project space for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Keep discussions technical and relevant to the project.
- Assume good intent, but accept corrections when something is unclear or harmful.
- Report security issues responsibly instead of posting exploit details publicly.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Spam, trolling, or intentionally disruptive comments.
- Sharing malicious instructions or exploit details in public issues.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove comments and may block users who repeatedly violate this code. Serious violations can result in immediate removal from project spaces.

## Contact

For conduct or security concerns, contact the maintainers through the repository owner profile or a private channel listed by the project.
